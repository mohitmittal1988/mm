01 / Research Focus

Explainable AI

Developing models that are not just accurate, but transparent and interpretable. Understanding how an AI makes decisions is crucial — especially in high-stakes fields like healthcare and finance.

Our work bridges the gap between black-box predictive power and human-understandable reasoning, ensuring AI systems remain accountable and trustworthy.

02 / Research Focus

Computer Vision

Developing algorithms that enable machines to interpret and understand visual information from the world. Our research spans object detection, image segmentation, and real-time video analysis.

We focus on building vision systems that are robust, efficient, and generalizable across diverse real-world conditions and domains.

03 / Research Focus

Disease Prediction

Predicting diseases in plants and humans using deep learning models. Our goal is to create accurate, timely detection systems that enable early intervention and prevention before conditions escalate.

By combining clinical data, imaging, and genetic markers, we develop multi-modal models that achieve state-of-the-art diagnostic accuracy.
