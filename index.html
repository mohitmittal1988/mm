Dr. Mohit Mittal

Data Scientist & AI Researcher

I am a Data Scientist with a Ph.D. in Computer Science and over 7 years of experience in Artificial Intelligence, specializing in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Explainable AI (XAI). My work focuses on designing end-to-end AI solutions, from predictive models to intelligent automation systems. I have a strong background in Natural Language Processing (NLP), Large Language Models (LLMs), and Computer Vision, with practical experience in LLM fine-tuning, cloud-based AI deployments (Azure, AWS), and MLOps. As a published researcher with over 1500 citations, I am passionate about bridging the gap between cutting-edge research and its practical application in real-world scenarios.
