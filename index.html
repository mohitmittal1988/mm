Bio

Mohit Mittal is a Data Scientist currently working at Shirateh Knowtion GmbH in Karlsruhe, Germany. He has spent 2 years as a post-doctoral researcher at INRIA, Lille, France, and completed his first post-doctorate in September 2019 from Kyoto Sangyo University, Kyoto, Japan. Mohit received his Doctorate degree from Gurukul Kangri University, India in 2018.

He has a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Guru Nanak Dev University, Amritsar in 2010 and 2011, respectively. His research interests include WSN, NLP, Data Mining, Explainable AI, Machine learning, and Deep learning. Mohit has published numerous papers in reputed journals and top-tier conferences.
